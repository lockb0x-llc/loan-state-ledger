The Agency Dilemma

The $5 Trillion "Telephone Game"

Information Asymmetry

In syndicated loans, the Agent Bank holds the "golden record." Meanwhile, Lenders and Borrowers operate in the dark, relying on delayed reports and static PDFs to understand their own positions.

The Agency Cost

The price of coordinating truth via email:

  • Hours spent reconciling Excel sheets vs. PDFs manually.
  • Delayed draws and payments due to state ambiguity.
  • Zero real-time visibility into risk, such as covenant breaches.

The Core Friction

Trust currently relies on manual verification, not cryptographic proof.

"When you have 50 lenders and 1 borrower managed by 1 agent, the cost of coordinating the truth is massive."
